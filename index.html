About Vignan's Institute of Information Technology

Vignan's Institute of Information Technology (VIIT) was established in 2002 in Visakhapatnam. It aims to cater to the educational needs of the general public and has acquired Autonomous status in 2017.

Vision

To be a premier institution in the field of education and research.

Mission

To provide quality education and promote research and innovation.
